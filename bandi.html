Moderato Band

The band's story

The story of Moderato Band began in 2017, when a group of experienced musicians decided to form a band with one main goal: to make people have fun and dance.

The shared passion for music and strong stage chemistry quickly created a recognizable sound and energetic performance style.

Over the years, the band has played at numerous weddings, clubs, and restaurants, always adapting its repertoire to suit the atmosphere of the occasion.
